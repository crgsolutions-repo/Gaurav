# Workplace Operations, Security, and Employment Policy
Category: Workplace Operations
Version: 1.0-demo

This demonstration policy requires company, security, HR, and legal review before adoption.

## Remote and hybrid work

Remote or hybrid work arrangements require manager approval where applicable. Employees remain responsible for working hours, information security, attendance requirements, and a safe workspace. Temporary remote work due to an emergency should be discussed with the manager or HR.

## Information security and acceptable use

Employees must protect credentials, use approved systems, report suspected phishing or data loss, and avoid uploading confidential company or personal data to unapproved services. Access to HR records follows least-privilege and minimum-necessary principles.

## Data privacy

Personal data should be collected only for a defined HR purpose, accessed by authorized roles, retained according to company requirements, and corrected when inaccurate. The HR assistant should not reveal coworker attendance, leave reasons, medical information, salary, complaints, or performance records to unauthorized employees.

## Workplace safety

Employees should report unsafe conditions, injuries, threats, or security concerns promptly. Immediate danger should be directed to local emergency services or workplace security before internal workflow completion.

## Performance and discipline

Performance expectations should be communicated clearly and reviewed using documented evidence. Corrective action should be proportionate, consistent, and provide the employee an opportunity to respond. Harassment reports, protected leave, good-faith complaints, and approved accommodations must not be treated as performance misconduct.

## Separation and exit

Resignation, retirement, termination, notice, final payroll, benefits transition, return of assets, and access removal should follow the applicable employment agreement and local requirements. The assistant may explain the company process but must not provide a definitive legal interpretation.
